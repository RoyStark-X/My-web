Projects

Land-Air UAV

This project focuses on the design of a miniature land-air robot for efficient and reliable material transportation. It features an innovative conversion mechanism composed of a ball screw nut pair and multiple gear sets, enabling quick and flexible transformation of flight rotor protectors into ground-traveling wheels. Additionally, statics and aerodynamics simulations were conducted to optimize the design, significantly reducing overall weight and air resistance while enhancing mobility and endurance.

Bird Nest

The BirdNest project addresses the challenges of the last mile in drone food delivery. It introduces an innovative system that acts as a delivery mailbox, allowing drones to deliver food directly to a recipient's window. With features like UV sterilization, extendable trays, and precise positioning through Bluetooth AoA technology, BirdNest ensures efficient, contactless, and hygienic delivery. This design reduces human labor, enhances food safety, and exemplifies the future of smart urban living by integrating automation with convenience.

Health Care Robot

The Family Healthcare Robot project, "Little Dingdang," is designed to address the challenges faced by elderly individuals living alone or those requiring long-term medication. This smart home medical companion robot integrates daily companionship, health monitoring, medication reminders, and emergency response functionalities. Equipped with advanced features such as IMU and binocular vision for precise indoor tracking and navigation, it ensures timely medication delivery and quick response to emergencies. With its user-friendly design, cost-effectiveness, and focus on elderly care, "Little Dingdang" offers significant social value and practical solutions to the pressing issues of an aging society.

Multi-Dimensional Wheel Hub Measurement System

This project introduces a non-contact measurement and analysis system based on image processing, designed to address the limitations of traditional manual inspection methods. By integrating CCD cameras, laser sensors, and advanced algorithms, the system enables high-precision, multi-feature measurements. It supports real-time quality control for large-scale industrial production, offering improved efficiency, accuracy, and versatility across diverse applications such as automotive manufacturing, electronics, and aerospace.

Haze Removal Guard

This project introduces the Haze Removal Guard, an innovative land-air tilt-rotor drone designed for efficient haze removal in both low and mid-to-high altitudes. The drone integrates tilt-rotor mechanisms, precise spraying systems, and autonomous navigation technologies to address current challenges in traditional haze removal methods.
