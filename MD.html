Project Overview

The Medical Companion Robot represents a breakthrough in elderly care technology, combining advanced robotics with healthcare solutions. Designed to address the growing needs of an aging population, this innovative system offers comprehensive support for independent living through automated medication management, real-time health monitoring, and emergency assistance.

Mission

To enhance the quality of life for elderly individuals through intelligent healthcare automation and reliable companionship.

Innovation

Integration of SLAM navigation, skeleton recognition, and smart medication dispensing in a unified platform.

Impact

Reducing caregiver burden while ensuring medication adherence and emergency response for seniors.

Core Features

Medication Management

Automated dispensing system with moisture-resistant storage and precise dosage control.

Health Monitoring

Real-time vital signs tracking and analysis for preventive healthcare.

Emergency Response

Intelligent fall detection and rapid assistance delivery system.

Navigation Intelligence

Advanced SLAM technology for precise indoor mapping and movement.

Robot Demonstration

The Medical Companion Robot showcases its real-world functionality through precise medication dispensing, autonomous navigation, and emergency assistance. Using advanced mapping and skeleton tracking, the robot delivers medication directly to users while avoiding obstacles. Its seamless performance highlights its reliability and practical application in home healthcare.

Medication Management

The robot features an automatic dispensing system with precise dosage control, preventing issues related to moisture, mold, and expired medications.

Navigation

The Medical Companion Robot leverages advanced technologies to ensure accurate, reliable, and efficient operation. Its navigation system uses Gmapping SLAM for real-time indoor mapping and autonomous path planning, enabling it to adapt to dynamic environments and avoid obstacles. Skeleton recognition technology identifies and tracks the user’s position by detecting up to 20 joint points, ensuring precise delivery of medication. Communication with a wearable device allows the robot to respond to emergencies instantly, combining LiDAR, IMU sensors, and real-time alerts for seamless functionality. These cutting-edge features make it a robust and intelligent solution for home healthcare.
